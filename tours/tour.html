Experience the spiritual awakening of Varanasi with a serene morning boat ride along the holy river, witnessing ancient rituals and breathtaking sunrise views.

Tour Itinerary & Points

  • 1

    Start Point: Dasashwamedh Ghat

    Time: 5:00 AM (Before Sunrise)

    Meeting: Our guide will meet you at the main entrance with a welcome kit including bottled water and traditional prayer lamp.

  • 2

    Boat Departure & Sunrise Viewing

    Duration: 45 minutes

    Activity: Board our traditional wooden boat. Experience the magical moment as the sun rises over the Ganges, creating golden reflections on the water. Perfect for photography.

  • 3

    Break Point: Assi Ghat

    Time: 5:45 AM - 6:15 AM

    Activity: Disembark for 30 minutes. Witness morning yoga sessions, enjoy masala chai from local vendors, and visit the small Shiva temple. Restroom facilities available.

  • 4

    Ghat Tour & Ritual Observation

    Duration: 1 hour

    Activity: Continue boat ride past Manikarnika and Scindia Ghats. Observe morning rituals, bathing traditions, and hear explanations of each ghat's significance from our guide.

  • 5

    End Point: Man Mandir Ghat

    Time: 7:15 AM

    Final Activity: Participate in a brief Ganga Aarti ceremony. Receive blessing from priest. Tour concludes with breakfast recommendations and assistance with return transportation.
