# 🌸 Varanasi Local Guide

Welcome to **Varanasi Local Guide** — your ultimate travel companion to explore the spiritual and cultural beauty of **Varanasi (Banaras)**, the city of light and life.  
This website helps travelers discover **famous ghats, temples, local food, traditions, and tours** in one place.

---

## 🌆 About the Project
**Varanasi Local Guide** is a responsive website built to help tourists and locals explore the essence of Varanasi through:
- Detailed information about **ghats, temples, and attractions**
- Local tips for **tours, walks, and boat rides**
- A user-friendly interface for **exploring and booking experiences**
- Contact options for local guidance and inquiries
